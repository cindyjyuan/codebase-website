ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO

CodeBase is one of Berkeley's first technical consulting groups, spearheading technical challenges for high-growth startups. CodeBase helps companies bridge technical gaps by developing mobile and web applications, assisting companies in setting up scalable infrastructure, providing code review/application testing, and implementing future product features for research and evaluation.

OUR CULTURE

Besides our shared passion for coding, we are a family that loves to get food and boba and go on retreats together.

PROJECTS

Each semester we take on projects solving technical challenges for startups, ranging from mobile and app development to IoT, machine learning, and many more.

COMMITTEES

We have four committees -- Finance, Marketing, Professional Development, and Internal Relations -- to further develop our members professionally and allow them to take an active part in helping the organization run smoothly, whether it be managing finances, recruiting tips, event-planning, and more.
