Work With CodeBase

As a Computer Science consulting organization, CodeBase aims to offer quality feedback on the needs and objectives of early-stage startups based in the Bay Area. For startups without technical talent, or for those in need of more, transforming a company from an idea to a business can be difficult. CodeBase alleviates growing pains and accelerates early growth by helping startups refine and advance their technical plan.

Typical partnerships begin with a pro-bono consultation meeting in which CodeBase members will assess the client’s existing technical vision, their development capabilities, and their future technical requirements/timeline. After this initial meeting, CodeBase will provide clients with a report including recommended follow-up steps.This report will include feedback regarding your team’s development capabilities based on an analysis of prior work and the size of your technical team. CodeBase will factor in your product development timeline and provide a plan to help you stay on track. Follow-up consultations related to your technical vision will primarily be focused on helping you select the technologies you need to create your product.

After consulting with us, you can take one of two paths. If CodeBase has the internal capacity to complete development work for the organization, we can work together create a semester-long project where CodeBase will develop your idea and give you the tools you need to operate as an independent entity. If CodeBase does not have the capacity to develop your project or you are looking to hire full time developers, we can connect you with our large network of local developers who will more closely match your needs.At CodeBase, we help startups turn their visions into concrete products fit to succeed. Whether you need guidance on designing your idea, technical development, or hiring developers, CodeBase can provide a pertinent consulting solution.

CodeBase gets it’s funding almost entirely from the generous donations of the clients we consult with. If you are interested in helping us out, you can donate on our website here.
