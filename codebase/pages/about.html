About Us

Mission

CodeBase is a non-profit UC Berkeley student organization that develops and designs software, websites, and apps for companies with a vision. We will improve our community by helping businesses take their innovative ideas to concrete production. Furthermore, we will create a fast-paced, project-based working environment for Berkeley students.

Work With Us

As a Computer Science consulting organization, CodeBase aims to offer quality feedback on the needs and objectives of early-stage startups based in the Bay Area. For startups without technical talent, or for those in need of more, transforming a company from an idea to a business can be difficult. CodeBase alleviates growing pains and accelerates early growth by helping startups refine and advance their technical plan.
